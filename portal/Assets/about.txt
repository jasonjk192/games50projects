Gameplay

Collect 3 spheres and jump into the pipe to finish the game
Player must grab the blue sphere to trigger the red collectibles to spawn inside the room
Red spheres are visible (and collectible) when the blue sphere is in close distance to them
Falling out of the map will cause player to respawn
Jumping into the pipe without all 3 red spheres causes player to respawn
